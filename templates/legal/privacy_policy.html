Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At NyumbaFinder KE, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and international best practices.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you:

  • Create an account
  • Complete your profile
  • Post or search for properties
  • Contact support

This may include your name, email, phone number, and identification details required for verification.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Verify landlords and agents
  • Prevent fake listings and scams
  • Improve our platform
  • Communicate important updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Download your data

Data Security

We take strong measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Only authorized personnel can access user data for verification and support purposes.

No Selling of Data

We will NEVER sell your personal information to third parties.

Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy:

Email: privacy@nyumbafinder.ke
